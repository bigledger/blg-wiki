## Design Elements

### **Typography**
- **Title Font**: Bold, 48px, Dark Gray `#1A1A1A`
- **Section Headers**: Bold, 28px, Section Color
- **Subheadings**: Bold, 18px, Dark Gray `#333`
- **Body Text**: Regular, 14px, Dark Gray `#666`
- **Feature Names**: Bold, 16px, Accent Color

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary Red (Problems)**: `#C41E3A`
- **Primary Green (Solutions)**: `#2D8F2D`
- **Primary Blue (Features)**: `#0052CC`
- **Primary Gold (Users)**: `#D4A500`
- **Dark Text**: `#1A1A1A` / `#333333`
- **Light Text**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Backgrounds**: Corresponding light shades with 20% opacity

### **Icons**
- **Style**: Flat design or line-based
- **Size**: 48px Ã— 48px for large icons, 24px Ã— 24px for small
- **Sources**: FontAwesome, Material Icons, or similar
- **Colors**: Match section accent colors

### **Spacing & Layout**
- **Padding**: 40px outer margin, 20px internal padding
- **Gap between sections**: 30px
- **Gap between blocks**: 15px
- **Corner radius**: 8px for boxes, 4px for text containers

### **Visual Enhancements**
- **Shadows**: Subtle drop shadow (2px offset, 4px blur, 10% black)
- **Borders**: 2px solid color for section borders
- **Transitions**: Optional fade or hover effects if designed for digital use
- **Icons**: Color-coded to match section (red for problems, green for solutions, etc.)
