## How to Manage the Structure and Design of Your Store

Designing your CP-Commerce marketplace is now easier than ever. With our CP-Commerce Admin Applet, you can create a stunning online store in a short time, without needing any IT background or programming skills. Our applet offers an intuitive interface that allows you to effortlessly customize your marketplace. Whether you're adding products, managing promotions, or optimizing your store for search engines, our user-friendly tools make the process seamless. Join the ranks of successful online entrepreneurs and bring your marketplace vision to life with CP-Commerce Admin Applet.

This comprehensive guide will empower you to build your e-commerce website or online marketplace with ease.

### Key Features:

-   **Layout Instances for Device-Specific Optimization**: Design web page structures tailored to different devices for optimal viewing experiences.
-   **Node-Based Structure**: Construct layouts with rows, columns, and widgets for flexible content organization.
-   **Versatile Widgets**: Add a wide range of customizable components, extending functionality from headers and image displays to product listings and sales order management.
-   **Advanced Configuration Options**: Tailor each widget to meet your specific needs.

### Additional Tips:

-   **Experiment with Node Combinations**: Try different combinations of rows, columns, and widgets to achieve unique and effective layouts across devices.
-   **Refer to Widget Documentation**: Consult the widget documentation for detailed instructions on configuring individual widgets and their settings. Widgets add essential content and functionality to your website. Each widget offers unique customization options to match your design and content need.
-   **Utilize Grid-Based Structure**: Create visually appealing and organized layouts using a grid-based approach.
-   **Ensure Responsive Design**: Optimize your website for optimal viewing across various devices.

### 12. iFrame Display

**Purpose**

Embed external content seamlessly within your website, expanding content offerings and potentially increasing user engagement. Enhance website functionality by integrating external tools, applications, or media players without requiring users to navigate away from your site. Facilitate collaboration with external content creators by showcasing their work within your website.

**Key Features**

-   **Dynamic Content Embedding**: Integrates external content from another website using the `<iframe>` HTML element, ensuring a smooth visual transition.
-   **Customizable Appearance**: Allows adjustments to the iframe's size, position, scrolling behavior, and borders to match your website's design.
-   **Responsive Design (Optional)**: Adapts the iframe's dimensions to different screen sizes and devices, maintaining optimal viewing experience across platforms.

**Configuration Options**

-   **Source URL**: Specify the web address of the content you want to embed within the iframe.
-   **Dimensions**: Define the width and height of the iframe to control the size of the embedded content.
-   **Scrolling**: Choose whether to display scrollbars within the iframe, allowing users to navigate lengthy content within the embed.
-   **Security**: Implement robust security measures to ensure only authorized websites are embedded and prevent malicious code injection.

**Example Use Cases**

-   **Video Embeds**: Showcase video content from platforms like YouTube or Vimeo directly within your website pages without requiring users to navigate away.
-   **Interactive Widgets**: Integrate interactive elements like polls, quizzes, or calculators from external providers to enhance user engagement.
-   **Social Media Feeds**: Display live social media feeds (e.g., Twitter, Instagram) within your website to connect with your audience and showcase real-time content.
-   **Maps and Location Embeds**: Embed interactive maps from Google Maps or other providers to showcase your physical location or provide directions to your visitors.
-   **Custom Applications**: Integrate custom applications or tools developed by other parties within your website for specific functionalities.

**Additional Notes**

-   Ensure the embedded content complements your website's overall design and user experience.
-   Be mindful of potential performance impacts and optimize iframe usage to avoid website loading delays.
-   Consider accessibility aspects by providing alternative text descriptions for iframes and ensuring they function properly with assistive technologies.
-   Respect the terms of service of the websites you are embedding content from.
